Our story

The hardest part of parenthood nobody talks about

You love your child completely. But somewhere between the nappy bag and the third cold coffee, something went missing — you.

Existing cafés offer a plastic toy box shoved in the corner. Parents deserve better. We're building a beautifully designed third space where the play infrastructure is woven into the architecture itself — and where adults are treated like adults.

"Not a café with a toy box. A lounge with a world built into it."

Sustainable play

Designed for wonder.
Built to last.

Every element chosen with intention — beautiful, safe, and kind to the planet.

FSC-certified timber

Every climbing structure, shelf, and toy crafted from responsibly sourced wood. Zero plastics in the play zone.

Safety-first design

Rounded edges, non-toxic finishes, soft-play integrated flooring. Certified to EU toy safety standard EN 71.

The adult lounge

Specialty coffee, seasonal menu, and actual comfortable seating — with sightlines to the play area throughout.

Biophilic architecture

Natural light, living walls, and materials that breathe. Designed by architects who are parents themselves.

Age-zoned spaces

Dedicated zones for crawlers, toddlers, and early school-age. No big kids trampling the littles.

Community programming

Workshops, parent circles, music mornings, birthday celebrations — woven into the weekly calendar.
